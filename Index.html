1. The highest mountain in the world in nature is Mauna Kea, not Mount Everest

Mount Everest image by https://media.istockphoto.com

We all know that in school we learned that the tallest mountain in the world is Mount Everest, but that's a lie, the tallest mountain in the world is Mauna Kea Mountain situated in Hawaii with an altitude below the surface of 5,998 meters and 4,205 meters above the surface with a total of 10,203 meters.

So tell your teachers they are wrong :)


2. Glass is neither a solid nor a liquid



Do you know that glass is neither a solid nor a liquid? The basic component of glass is silica (SiO2), which is mixed with other components, such as soda and limestone, at temperatures around 1700¬∞C. This process results in a viscous, yellowish liquid mixture that resembles honey. On cooling, the mixture hardens, becoming a solid.

According to it's molecular structure, glass is classified neither as a liquid nor as a solid, but as a non-crystalline solid or simply an amorphous solid. Solid crystals are those that show a pattern of symmetry in their molecular composition. As glass does not form this pattern in its structure, it is understood to be non-crystalline or amorphous.


3. African buffalo make decisions by voting



Apparently, democracy is not limited to humanity. For example, African buffalo are known to select which direction they will continue to vote. One by one, the adult females in the herd get up, look in a specific direction and then lie down. The direction that attracts the most attention is often where the herd travels. If the vote is heavily divided, the herd will usually split temporarily.
